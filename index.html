THE AKAN NAMING HISTORY

The Akan are a meta-ethnicity living primarily in
GHANA and IVORY COAST. The Akan language are a group
of dialects within the central Tano branch of the
Potou-Tano sub family of the Niger-Congo family.

The Akan people name their children after the day
of the week they were born and their order which
they were born.

This "day name" have further meaning concerning
the soul and the character of the person.
